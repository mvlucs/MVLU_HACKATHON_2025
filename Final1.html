Bio Extracts & Organic Solutions

Neem Extract

Natural pesticide effective against multiple pests. Mix 50ml with 1 liter of water and spray on crops.

Garlic-Chili Spray

Organic solution to deter insects. Crush 100g garlic and 100g chili, mix with 1 liter water, strain and spray.

Compost Tea

Nutrient-rich liquid fertilizer. Steep compost in water for 3-5 days, dilute 1:10 with water before use.

Seaweed Extract

Growth promoter rich in micronutrients. Dilute 20ml per liter of water and spray on leaves.

How to Use Bio Extracts

Bio extracts are concentrated natural solutions made from plants, herbs, and other organic materials. They provide an eco-friendly alternative to chemical pesticides and fertilizers.

Application Guidelines:

  • Always dilute extracts as per instructions
  • Test on a small area before full application
  • Apply during cooler parts of the day (early morning or late evening)
  • Store in cool, dark places away from direct sunlight
  • Use within 2-3 weeks of preparation for best results

Crop Disease Gallery

Wheat Rust

Symptoms: Orange-brown pustules on leaves and stems

Treatment: Apply fungicides containing triazoles. Remove and destroy infected plants.

Rice Blast

Symptoms: Diamond-shaped lesions with gray centers and brown borders

Treatment: Use resistant varieties. Apply silica fertilizers. Fungicide spray with tricyclazole.

Leaf holes

Symptoms: Large, distorted galls on ears, tassels, stalks and leaves

Treatment: Use neem oil spray or Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) and remove visible pests to control leaf holes..

Cotton Root Rot

Symptoms: Sudden wilting and death of plants, reddish-brown discoloration of vascular tissue

Treatment: Soil solarization. Improve drainage. Use resistant varieties.

Preventive Measures

Prevention is always better than cure when it comes to crop diseases. Follow these practices to minimize disease outbreaks:

  • Use certified disease-free seeds
  • Practice crop rotation to break disease cycles
  • Maintain proper plant spacing for good air circulation
  • Monitor fields regularly for early signs of disease
  • Remove and destroy infected plant material
  • Keep irrigation water off leaves when possible
  • Use balanced fertilization to maintain plant health

Weather Advisory for Farmers

Weather plays a crucial role in agricultural success. Here are some general guidelines based on weather conditions:

During High Rainfall:

  • Ensure proper drainage in fields
  • Delay fertilizer application
  • Monitor for fungal diseases

During Drought Conditions:

  • Use drip irrigation to conserve water
  • Apply mulch to reduce evaporation
  • Consider drought-resistant crop varieties

During High Temperatures:

  • Water plants during cooler parts of the day
  • Provide shade for sensitive crops
  • Watch for heat stress symptoms
